Our Goal

We have set Goal what we can do for the helpless people. We have taken some steps towards this goal. The details in the below.

Safe Drinking Water

In Bangladesh there is so many place where people can't drink safe water. We are working to provide safe drinking water to the people. Also we told teach them how to purify their drinking water.

Child Care

Children are very important to a family, a community or a country. In our country many family can't provide nitritious food to the child. For their better life we provide nutritious food to those children.

Shelter for a Family

There is a huge number of people in Bangladesh who are homeless. They spent almost their whole life at a railway staion. We find those people and give them a shelter where they can spent their life happily.

No More Hunger, food for everyone

Many people in Bangladesh spent below poverty line life. Many of them can't get food three times a day. So, most of them eat only once in a day. We are trying to help those people so that no one can remain hungry.
